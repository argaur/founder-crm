Three steps. That's it.

Zero training for reps
01
Forward

A rep forwards the conversation

A WhatsApp chat, a voice note recorded after a site visit, or a screenshot of an email — sent to the team's Telegram bot. That is the entire data-entry workflow.

02
Extract

AI reads it like a rep would

The model pulls out the company, contact, seat count, city, space type, budget per seat, and move-in date — and stages the lead from Inquiry to Negotiation based on what was actually said.

03
Run

The pipeline runs itself

The lead lands staged, valued, and assigned. If it goes quiet past its stage threshold, the rep gets nudged with full context. The manager's funnel updates without anyone filing a report.
