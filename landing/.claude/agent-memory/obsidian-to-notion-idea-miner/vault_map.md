# Obsidian Vault Map

**Vault root:** G:\My Drive\Obsidian Vault\

## High-Yield Zones (always scan)

| Folder / File | Why Productive |
|---|---|
| Knowledge/Newsletter Insights/YYYY-MM-DD.md | Claude-extracted insights from newsletters, already structured with data points. Strong on specificity. |
| Knowledge/Courses/Rethink Systems/*.md | Active PM course notes — case studies, PRD lessons, research findings. Gaurav's direct work. |

## Structural Conventions Gaurav Uses

- `> Migrated from Notion · date · Source: <name>` — Notion-migrated files, may duplicate Notion content
- `> Captured from Google Drive · date` — external doc captures, high-value (case study source)
- YAML frontmatter with `status:` and `tags:` — active project files
- `## Relationship to LinkedIn Content` section — explicit signal that note was already mapped to posts
- `#log` / `#capture` / `#idea` — Telegram capture tags referenced in notes
